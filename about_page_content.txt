About
LILIT
LILIT is a social enterprise dedicated to bridging educational gaps from rural villages to
                        urban communities. We focus on enhancing knowledge in information technology and languages,
                        including English, by identifying under served areas and creating meaningful learning opportunities.
Our primary goal is to empower school students, school leavers, and professionals across various
                        sectors to pursue higher education. What sets us apart is our ability to offer both online and
                        blended learning courses at affordable rates, making quality education accessible to all.
At LILIT, we design our courses based on real societal needs responding to language and technological
                        skill demands as well as business, institutional, and social requirements. Our step-by-step approach
                        ensures learners can progress to higher levels of knowledge and skill development, earning recognized
                        certifications along the way.
We also offer flexible learning solutions such as evening and fully online courses for individuals
                        with daytime commitments. Participants can join courses with discretion, maintaining privacy if
                        preferred.
By providing a structured, inclusive, and affordable educational experience, LILIT helps learners
                        reach their academic and professional goals through recognized qualifications and a supportive
                        learning environment.

Objective
Excellence in Education
To create an excellent educational environment that fosters motivation, guidance, and empowerment to achieve the best possible learning outcomes.
Universal Access
Providing access to education from any location and at any age, while ensuring standardized education under fair and affordable course fees.

Establishing a structured course framework that enables students to progress step by step from basic knowledge levels and minimum qualifications to higher education and degree levels.

Providing course opportunities that can be easily accessed by both school students and busy individuals, while also establishing a network of collaborative educational centers for practical training, where they can conveniently visit centers close to their preferred locations.
