- Content Creation: Generate blog posts, social media content, and scripts quickly.
- Image and Video Content Creation.
- Home & Garden decoration using AI.
- Budget management with AI.
- Makeup/Hair Virtual Try-Ons.
- Food recipe generation and daily home tasks using AI tools.

==========================================================================

COURSE OVERVIEW / SUBJECTS COVERED:

3. Basics of Electronics for Beginners.
4. Robotic Components and Hardware.
5. Robotic Sensors and Applications.

7. Hands-on Projects with Arduino.
